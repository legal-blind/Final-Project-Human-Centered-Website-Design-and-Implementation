FAQ & Help

Quick answers to common planning questions. Click a question to expand.

How do I book a recommended itinerary?
+

Use the itinerary as a checklist. Book flights and major transport first, then reserve key experiences. We suggest using a single booking site for flights to compare prices quickly.

Can you create a custom plan for me?
+

Yes. Use the contact form and include your dates, budget, and interests. We'll send a simple plan and suggestions.

Do you provide booking services?
+

No. We provide inspiration and plan outlines. Booking remains with you or your preferred travel agent.
