About this project

This guide focuses on clear, human-centered trip ideas for busy people. The goal: fewer tabs, faster decisions.

What we include

  • Short, scannable destination summaries.
  • Sample itineraries you can follow or adapt.
  • Practical tips: when to visit, what to pack, and local transport notes.

Design choices

Simple typography, consistent spacing, and predictable navigation reduce friction. Interactive previews and expandable FAQ let you control how much detail you see.
